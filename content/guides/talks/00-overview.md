# Overview

Giving a talk at VizChitra is an opportunity to share your ideas, insights, and experiences with a community of visualization practitioners.

> Stories are just data with a soul.
> — Brené Brown

## Intent

At VizChitra, talks are a **Narrative Journey** — spaces where lived experience becomes stories and these stories reshapes how we see our own work.

This guide helps you move from shaping a strong proposal, through review and preparation, to delivering a confident talk that feels clear, reflective, and meaningful.

## Formats of Talks

**Standard Talk** – A deep-dive presentation that allow you to explore complex topics throughly and share personal insights and lessons from your work.

_Duration_: 20 min presentation (+ 5 min Q&A + 5 min transition)

**Lightning Talk** – A fast-paced, focused presentations designed to deliver maximum impact in minimal time. Perfect for introducing novel concepts or sharing specific solutions.

_Duration_: 10 minutes presentation (+ 3 min Q&A + 2 min transition)
